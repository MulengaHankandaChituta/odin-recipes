Description

Indulge in the savory delight of our chicken and chips dish. a culinary masterpiece that promises a symphony of flavors and textures. Succulent, tender chicken seasoned to perfection, takes center stage in this mouth watering creation.

Accompanying this flavorful poultry sensation are a generous serving of golden crispy chips.Each chip is meticulously crafted to achieve, the ideal balance between a crispy exterior, and a fluffy tender interior sprinkled with just the right amount of salt and seasoning, our chips are a delightful compliment to the main star, creating a harmonious marriage of textures and tastes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces(Drumsticks or thighs)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Dried thyme
  • Oive or vegetable oil for cooking
  • Potatoes
  • Fresh parsley chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat oven or oil for frying
  2. Season the chicken
  3. Cook the chicken
  4. Prepare the potatoes
  5. Preheat oil for frying
  6. Cook the chips
  7. Garnish and serve
