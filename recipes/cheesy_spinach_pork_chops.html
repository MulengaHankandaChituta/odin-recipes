Cheesy Spinach Pork Chops

Description

Indulge your taste buds with the delectable, Cheesy Spinach Pork Chops. A culinary masterpiece that combines succulent pork chops with a rich and flavorful twist. Each tender pork chop is expertly seasoned and pan seared to perfection, creating a savoury foundation for the symphony of tastes to come.

The real star of the dish is our luscious cheese and spinach stuffing. Vibrant, nutrient packed spinach is delicately sauteed and blended with a melty, gooey medley of premium cheeses. This delightful mixture is generously spooned onto the pork chops, infusing them with a burst of creamy savory goodness.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless pork chops
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika(optional for added flavor)
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes(optional for hint of heat)
  • Fresh parsley chopped for garnish(optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: preheat your oven to 190°C
  2. Season the pork chops: Season both sides of the pork chops with salt and pepper
  3. Create a pocket: Using a knife carefully cut a pocket in each pork chop, make sure not to cut through the other side. This pocket will be filled with the cheesy spinach mixture.
  4. Prepare the filling: In a skillet heat the olive oil, on medium heat.Add minced garlic and saute until fragrant.Add chopped spinach, and cook until wilted.Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  5. Make the cheese and spinach mixture: In a bowl,combine the wilted spinach and mozzarella, parmesan, cream cheese,oregano, onion powder smoked paprika, and red pepper flakes. Mix well until all Ingredients are evenly combined.
  6. Sear the pork chops: In the same skillet or a new one Sear the stuffed pork chops over a medium-high heat for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they develop a golden brown crust.
  7. Transfer to oven: Place the seared pork chops on a baking dish and transfer them to the preheated oven.Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the pork reaches a safe internal temperature of 63°C.
  8. Garnish and serve: once cooked remove the pork chops from the oven garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired.Allow them to rest for a few minutes before serving.
  9. Enjoy: Serve the cheesy spinach pork chops hot,and savor the delicious combination of tender pork, creamy cheese and flavorful spinach. Enjoy your delightful and cheesy culinary creation.
