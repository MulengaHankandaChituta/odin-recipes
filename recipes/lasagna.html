Lasagna

Description

Indulge in the comforting layers, of a beef lasagna, A classic italian dish tha captivates with its rich and hearty flavors.

This culinary masterpiece features tender lasagna noodles, embraced by a luscious blend of seasoned ground beef, carmelized onions and garlic infused tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 450g lean ground beef
  • One large onion finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 cans (4 oz each) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can(6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 teasponn dried oreganos
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 a teaspoon dried thyme
  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked al dente
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • Fresh parsley, chopped(for garnish optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to (190°C)
  2. Prepare the meat sauce
  3. Prepare the cheese mixture
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Top the lasagna with cheese
  6. Bake in the oven
  7. Remove the foil and let it bake for 10-15 minutes till golden bubbly
  8. Let it rest
  9. Garnish and serve
